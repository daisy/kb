About

Version: WCAG 2.0 and up
Number: 2.3.2
Level: AAA
Applicability:
  • Animations
  • Video
Requirement:

Web pages do not contain anything that flashes more than three times in any one second period.
Guidance:
  • Understanding Success Criterion 2.3.2
  • How to meet Success Criterion 2.3.2
Techniques:
  • G19: Ensuring that no component of the content flashes more than three times in any 1-second period

Purpose

The purpose of this success criterion is to ensure that photosensitive readers are not put at risk of seizure when reading digital publications.

How to Meet

This success criterion differs from 2.3.1 Three Flashes or Below Threshold in that authors must ensure there is no content that flashes more than three times per second without exception.
